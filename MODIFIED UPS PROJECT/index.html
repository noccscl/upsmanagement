Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of UPS management services to ensure optimal performance and peace of mind.

Real-time Monitoring

Gain immediate insights into your UPS status, including power supply, battery level, and uptime.

Event Logs

Maintain logs of significant events like power outages and transitions between battery and normal operation.

Detailed Battery Health Reports

Generate reports on remaining capacity, runtime, charge/discharge cycles, internal resistance, and temperature.

Predictive Battery Maintenance

Analyze battery health data to predict failures and generate alerts for proactive battery replacement.

Battery Capacity Testing

Initiate controlled battery discharge tests to assess runtime capability and compare with historical data.

Customizable Alerts

Configure notifications for low battery levels, unexpected discharges, and other significant events.

Historical Data Analysis and Trending

Visualize battery health data over time to identify performance trends and plan maintenance schedules.

Integration with Third-Party Systems

Integrate with existing network monitoring platforms for centralized data collection and analysis.
